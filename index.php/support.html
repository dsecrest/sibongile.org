Sponsor-A-Child Program

Our “Sponsor a Child” Program is a great, sustainable way to provide for the daily needs of our children as well as a rewarding experience for our donors. By sponsoring a child, you agree to contribute a certain amount each month (currently R200, 20€ or 25US$). You receive a picture and details of your child, an update every six months and a notification should the health of your child change drastically. Join “Sponsor a Child” by simply contacting us.

Please note that due to their disabilities, our children are not able to respond personally (writing letters, etc).
